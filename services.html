Our Vetting Process

Every caregiver goes through our comprehensive 4-step verification process before joining our network.

1. Interview

In-depth personal interview assessing character and childcare philosophy.

2. Background Check

Criminal records, sex offender registry, driving records, and identity verification.

3. Training

CPR/First Aid certification, child development training, and safety protocols.

4. Placement

Careful family matching, trial visit, and ongoing support throughout the placement.
